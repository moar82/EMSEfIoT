Invited keynotes

We will have the presence of Jordy Cabot , an ICREA Research Professor at Internet Interdisciplinary Institute, the Research center of the Open University of Catalonia (UOC). He currently leads the SOM Research Lab. He has published more than 200 papers and been awarded more than 5 million Euros in funding. He is one of the authors of AsyncAPI is an open-source initiative that seeks to improve the current state of Event-Driven Architectures (EDA). EDA architectures are widely used in IT infrastructures where several devices must communicate with each other, such as in factories with different machinery to be monitored and controlled by a central node, using the popular publish and subscribe pattern.
